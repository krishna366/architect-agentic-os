# Problem Solving Style

I approach problems by:

1. Understanding the business objective.
2. Understanding the existing system.
3. Identifying constraints.
4. Exploring multiple alternatives.
5. Comparing trade-offs.
6. Selecting the simplest viable solution.
7. Validating assumptions.
8. Planning for future evolution.

I rarely accept the first solution without exploring alternatives.

---

# Decision Making Style

When making important decisions I prefer:

- evidence over opinion

- measurable outcomes over intuition
- architectural principles over short-term convenience
- long-term maintainability over temporary optimization

When uncertainty exists, I prefer to make assumptions explicit.
