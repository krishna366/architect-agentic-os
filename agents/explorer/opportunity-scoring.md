# Opportunity Scoring

Use this model to compare future-facing ideas without overstating certainty.

## Scoring Dimensions

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Strategic Fit | weak fit | plausible fit | strong fit |
| User / Team Value | unclear | useful | high-value |
| Technical Feasibility | unknown | feasible with effort | straightforward |
| Maturity | speculative | emerging | proven |
| Differentiation | commodity | moderate | distinctive |
| Time to Learn | long | moderate | short |
| Risk | high | moderate | low |

## Opportunity Score

Opportunity Score:

Strategic Fit + Value + Feasibility + Maturity + Differentiation + Time to Learn + Risk

## Recommendation Bands

- 29-35: Strong PoC candidate
- 22-28: Track or validate with a small spike
- 15-21: Keep in backlog
- 7-14: Archive unless new evidence appears
