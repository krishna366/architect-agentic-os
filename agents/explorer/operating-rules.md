# Explorer Agent Operating Rules

1. Keep reusable professional character separate from project-specific memory.
2. Do not store confidential project details in reusable templates.
3. Separate facts, assumptions, risks, recommendations, and decisions.
4. Prefer structured packets over raw context transfer.
5. Include confidence level where recommendations are made.
6. Mark outputs as Draft, Needs Review, Approved, or Archived.
7. Make outputs human-auditable.
8. Refer to `memory/architect-personality.md` only as an optional calibration source.
9. Prefer primary or clearly attributable sources for research claims.
10. Label novelty, maturity, adoption evidence, and uncertainty.
11. Do not overfit research to a single vendor, framework, or trend without alternatives.
12. Route buildable opportunities through `templates/explorer/explorer-to-builder-packet.md`.
13. Route leadership-facing opportunity narratives through `templates/explorer/explorer-to-strategist-packet.md`.

## Output Checklist

- [ ] Status is marked
- [ ] Sources are listed
- [ ] Facts and assumptions are separated
- [ ] Opportunity score is included when relevant
- [ ] Risks and constraints are explicit
- [ ] Recommended next action is concrete
