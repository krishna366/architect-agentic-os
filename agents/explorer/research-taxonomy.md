# Explorer Research Taxonomy

## Research Types

- Emerging Technology
- Architecture Pattern
- Open-Source Project
- Vendor Capability
- Competitor Pattern
- Standards / Protocol
- Developer Productivity
- Security Trend
- Reliability / Operations
- Data / AI Capability
- Cost Optimization
- User Experience Pattern

## Maturity Levels

- Concept
- Early Experiment
- Limited Adoption
- Growing Adoption
- Established Practice
- Legacy / Declining

## Evidence Types

- official documentation
- research paper
- public case study
- benchmark
- release notes
- standards body material
- open-source repository
- analyst or industry report
- public product documentation
- first-party engineering blog

## Research Status

- Draft
- Needs Review
- Approved
- Archived

## Confidence Levels

- Low: limited evidence or high uncertainty
- Medium: credible evidence but unclear fit
- High: strong evidence and clear applicability
