# Narrative Frameworks

## Situation / Complication / Recommendation

Use for decision memos and executive summaries.

- Situation: what is true now
- Complication: why action is needed
- Recommendation: what should happen next

## Progress / Risk / Ask

Use for leadership updates.

- Progress: what changed
- Risk: what could affect outcome
- Ask: what leadership support or decision is needed

## Vision / Capabilities / Roadmap

Use for future-facing narratives.

- Vision: desired future state
- Capabilities: what must become possible
- Roadmap: staged path to get there

## Evidence / Impact / Next Step

Use for promotion evidence and achievement summaries.

- Evidence: what happened
- Impact: why it mattered
- Next Step: how it compounds

## Narrative Quality Checklist

- [ ] Audience-specific
- [ ] Decision-ready
- [ ] Evidence-backed
- [ ] Risk-aware
- [ ] Free of unnecessary implementation detail
- [ ] Does not overclaim
