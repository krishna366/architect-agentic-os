# Stakeholder Model

## Stakeholder Types

- Executive Leader
- Product Leader
- Engineering Leader
- Architecture Peer
- Delivery Team
- Security / Compliance Partner
- Operations Partner
- Customer-Facing Partner
- Hiring / Promotion Reviewer

## Stakeholder Needs

| Stakeholder | Usually Cares About | Preferred Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Executive Leader | outcomes, risk, investment, trade-offs | executive summary |
| Product Leader | customer value, roadmap, sequencing | roadmap narrative |
| Engineering Leader | feasibility, delivery risk, ownership | leadership update |
| Architecture Peer | trade-offs, constraints, design quality | decision memo |
| Delivery Team | scope, dependencies, next steps | implementation narrative |
| Promotion Reviewer | evidence, scope, influence, impact | promotion evidence |
