# Risk Severity Model

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Meaning | Typical Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | likely major outage, data exposure, irreversible decision, or severe business impact | stop and escalate |
| High | material risk to delivery, reliability, security, or maintainability | require changes |
| Medium | meaningful concern with available mitigation | address or accept explicitly |
| Low | minor issue or improvement opportunity | suggest improvement |
| Informational | context, note, or future consideration | record only |

## Likelihood Levels

- Rare
- Possible
- Likely
- Expected

## Risk Statement Format

Use this format:

Because `<condition>`, there is a `<likelihood>` chance of `<impact>`, affecting `<system/user/team>`.
